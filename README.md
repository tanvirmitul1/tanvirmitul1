## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Full Stack Engineer** based in Bangladesh, currently leading frontend development on large-scale, AI-driven national platforms at **Dream71 Bangladesh Limited** under the ICT Division. I build fast, accessible interfaces and connect them to real ML/NLP systems — machine translation, speech-to-text, and document intelligence — through clean, well-designed APIs.

- 🔭 Currently building **[Kagoj.ai](https://kagoj.ai/)** & **[Jiggasha.ai](https://jiggasha.ai/)**
- 🧠 Focused on the **Machine Translator Component** (`MT_SD-15`, EBLICT, BCC) — multilingual NLP pipelines
- 🎨 Passionate about clean UI/UX, performant frontends, and thoughtful component architecture
- 🌱 Always exploring new tools in the React/Next.js and AI-integration space
- 💬 Ask me about **Next.js, React, TypeScript, Laravel, or AI product integration**
